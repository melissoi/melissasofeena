HOW IT STARTED?

It is all started when I aced my UPSR with flying colours. And my mom suggested me to apply for MRSM, and when it got approved, I had to be interviewed at MRSM Terendak, instead of other MRSM. I still remember the teachers that interviewed me that time. It was such a wonderful memory. After being accepted to study there, we were divided by homeroom, and my homeroom that time was "Phoenix B" which has a few boys and girls. During my introduction time with my homeroommates and the teacher, I've got to know that my homeroom teacher has the same birthdate as mine. What a coincidence! My homeroommates also are nice. What a luck!

MY CLASSMATES!

Since we were divided by homeroom, so our classmates also based on homerooms. So, my class was 1B3 which consist of Phoenix A and Phoenix B homerooms. The class system was divided by section, side A which consist of 1A1, 1A2, 1A3. and side B have 1B1, 1B2 and 1B3. We have unlimited of memories when we were classmates. We were unseperatable. Bonds within classmates and the batch clicked sooner than I thought. We may have ups and down as classmates and batchmates, but we did meetings and stuffs to ensure we solve the misunderstanding. Being here teaches me to be more mature and considerate as a person.

Before i forgot to mention, usually form 1 students were blessed with having a very beautiful rooms and dorms. So, here I got a nice room with my other 7 roommates. i still remember that we 7 roommates discussed to join the same uniform for curricular which is taekwondo. So when it comes to curricular, we usually go together. But at that time, my uniform which is 'Taekwondo' was not active as they are now. We rarely have competitions, grading or something. Kindly said, i somehow thought i wasted my 3 years of joining 'Taekwondo'. But i will update about how 'Taekwondo' got changed when I started form 4.

PEOPLE COME AND GO

The saddest moment of my life so far. After all of us struggling to achieve flying colours for our PT3, we had to say goodbye for those who got accepted to go further by moving to other school. I had to say goodbye to half of my batchmates as they parted ways to go to various MRSM like Tun Ghaffar Baba (TGB) and Taiping. Before that, we do some activities together while waiting for our PT3 results. We somehow managed to watch movies together almost every night in surau, we managed to enter the soldiers camp beside our school. Also, we got free tickets to watch football match! It was really awesome. Im glad we managed to do some great memories before we actually bid goodbye.

I still remember, after we received the names that was accepted to move school, all of us was crying so hard inside the library. And then the teacher played sad songs and almost all of us sobbing so hard. Its funny yet so sad.
